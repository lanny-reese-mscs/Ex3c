Lighting

ADJ Stinger

The ADJ Stinger is a three in one, light fixture. It has a ring of strobe light around the edge with different patterns to choose from. A retating RGB color light in middle what adds a realy cool effect. Last it has a red and green laser right in the middle that can cover the whole room.

ADJ Nucleus Pro

The ADJ Nucleus Pro is my center light which has 6 moving heads and can all move separately. Three of them are mirror tilt plates that tilt front and back and also, left and right. The other three are mirror tubes that can spin and twist.
